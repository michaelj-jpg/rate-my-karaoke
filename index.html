💡 How to Simulate the Experience

  • 1. Host stage setup: Under the Host Console, select a contestant (e.g. Tyler Robinson).
  • 2. Real-time sync: Observe the Guest Phone on the right instantly updates its active performer header!
  • 3. Cast anonymous votes: On the Guest Phone, adjust the sliders for Lyric Accuracy, Tone Quality, and Crowd Reaction, then click Submit Anonymous Rating.
